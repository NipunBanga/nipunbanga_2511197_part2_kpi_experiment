# Executive Recommendation Memo

## Executive Summary

This memo evaluates the A/B experiment conducted on the redesigned
onboarding and activation experience for a subscription-based digital
product. The objective is to determine whether the Treatment experience
should be deployed based on business performance, statistical evidence,
guardrail metrics, and segment-level insights.

## Business Background

The onboarding journey directly influences activation, paid conversion,
engagement, and recurring revenue. The redesigned experience aims to
improve these outcomes while maintaining a healthy customer experience.

## Experiment Objective

Determine whether the Treatment onboarding experience should replace the
existing Control onboarding flow.

## North Star Metric

**Paid Conversion Rate**

This metric best reflects sustainable revenue generation because it
measures successful conversion of users into paying subscribers.

## Supporting KPIs

-   Landing Page Visit Rate
-   Trial Start Rate
-   Onboarding Completion Rate
-   ARPU
-   ARPPU
-   Engagement Score
-   Days to Convert

## Guardrail Metrics

-   Refund Rate
-   Support Ticket Rate
-   Revenue Quality
-   Segment Performance
-   Engagement Score

## Decision Matrix

  Scenario                                       Recommendation
  ---------------------------------------------- ------------------------------
  Significant improvement + healthy guardrails   Launch
  Improvement with segment variation             Launch for selected segments
  Inconclusive evidence                          Continue testing
  Negative business impact                       Do not launch

## Risk Assessment

-   Customer Experience Risk
-   Operational Risk
-   Financial Risk
-   Statistical Risk
-   Segment Risk

## Monitoring Plan

Monitor Paid Conversion Rate, ARPU, Refund Rate, Support Ticket Rate,
Engagement Score, Days to Convert, and Segment Performance after
rollout.

## Conclusion

A production rollout should only occur when statistically significant
improvement is accompanied by stable guardrail metrics and positive
business outcomes.
